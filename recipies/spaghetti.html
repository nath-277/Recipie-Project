Spaghetti

Description

This spaghetti recipe is a simple and delicious dish featuring a savory tomato sauce, tender pasta, and a hint of garlic and herbs.

Ingredients

  • 12 oz spaghetti
  • 2 cups marinara sauce
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Grated Parmesan cheese, for serving

Steps

  1. Cook spaghetti according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add chopped onion and garlic, and saut√© until softened.
  3. Add ground beef to the skillet and cook until browned. Drain excess fat.
  4. Stir in marinara sauce, oregano, and basil. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  5. Add cooked spaghetti to the sauce and toss to combine.
  6. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  7. Serve hot, topped with grated Parmesan cheese.
