Lasagna

Description

This lasagna recipe is a classic Italian dish with layers of pasta, meat sauce, and cheese, baked to perfection.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 3 cups mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • 2 cups marinara sauce
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook lasagna noodles according to package instructions.
  3. In a large skillet, cook ground beef and garlic until browned. Drain excess fat.
  4. Stir in marinara sauce and simmer for 10 minutes.
  5. Spread a thin layer of sauce on the bottom of a baking dish.
  6. Layer noodles, ricotta, mozzarella, and sauce. Repeat layers.
  7. Top with remaining mozzarella and Parmesan cheese.
  8. Bake for 25-30 minutes, until bubbly and golden.
  9. Let stand for 10 minutes before serving.
