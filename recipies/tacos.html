Tacos

Description

These tacos are bursting with flavor, featuring seasoned ground beef, fresh toppings, and a zesty lime squeeze.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 packet taco seasoning
  • 12 small corn tortillas
  • 1 cup shredded lettuce
  • 1 cup diced tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped cilantro
  • 1 lime, cut into wedges
  • Sour cream, for serving
  • Hot sauce, for serving

Steps

  1. In a skillet, cook ground beef over medium heat until browned. Drain excess fat.
  2. Stir in taco seasoning and a small amount of water according to package instructions. Simmer until thickened.
  3. Warm the tortillas in a dry skillet or microwave.
  4. Assemble the tacos by placing the seasoned beef in each tortilla.
  5. Top with shredded lettuce, diced tomatoes, cheese, and cilantro.
  6. Squeeze lime juice over the top and serve with sour cream and hot sauce.
